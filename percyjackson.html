Percy Jackson is a movie about a boy named Percy who discovers he is the son of Greek god Poseidon. He befriends Annabeth and embarks on a journey with his friends to retrieve the Golden Fleece to save a magical tree containing the spirit of Zeus's daughter Thalia Grace who died at the gates of Camp Half-Blood while saving Annabeth, Luke and Grover. Along the way, Percy finds himself caught between angry and battling gods and discovers that his mother is in the Underworld with Hades. He decides to travel on a dangerous quest to retrieve the lightning bolt and save his mother.
